Hi, my name is Bita.
I have been interested in robotics for as long as I can remember. In 2016, I entered Qazvin Islamic Azad University in the field of Robotics engineering. And after 2 months, I joined the MRL HSL Lab. MRL HSL is a robotics research team that competes in the RoboCup in the Humanoid Robot League every year. the goal and dream of RoboCup is designing robots in 2050 that can play soccer with human.
My experiences are mostly about designing, developing and control electronic embedded systems, such as Servo Driver Motor. I also have practical knowledge about low-frequency circuit and PCB design, Hardware Debugging, Soldering, Simulation and Microcontroller programming(ARM & AVR).
